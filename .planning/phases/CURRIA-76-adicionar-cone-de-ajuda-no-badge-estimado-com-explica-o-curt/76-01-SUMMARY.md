# 76-01 Summary

Phase 76 made the ATS Readiness `Estimado` badge easier to understand without changing any scoring logic.

## What changed

- added a shared ATS readiness status badge component
- `Estimado` now shows a compact `?` help affordance
- the tooltip explains the estimated range in plain PT-BR
- `Final` remains visually clean and unchanged
- the comparison view and session list now share the same behavior

## Outcome

Users no longer need to guess what `Estimado` means. The explanation is now available at the exact point of doubt, without polluting the rest of the score UI.
