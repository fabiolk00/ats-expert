## Decisions Made

- Assessment-aware targeting plans use adapter-derived compatibility fields instead of recalculating score, coverage, low-fit, evidence, or permissions in `build-targeting-plan.ts`.
- Target role extraction inside `evaluateJobCompatibility` is intentionally generic: explicit labels resolve as heuristic/high; otherwise it falls back to `Vaga Alvo` with fallback/low.
- The deterministic score keeps detailed audit data under `audit.dimensionDetails`, while display-facing adapters consume the flat top-level `dimensions`, `counts`, and `weights`.
